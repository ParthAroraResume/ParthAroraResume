// 01 — About Me

The Human Behind the Resume

🔧 ISTP — The Virtuoso

Hi, I'm Parth. According to personality science — and honestly, everyone who's worked with me — I'm an ISTP: The Virtuoso. That means I'm the person in the room who's already figured out how to fix the problem while everyone else is still making a slide deck about it.

ISTPs are bold, practical, and quietly brilliant. I don't overthink. I observe, I analyze, I act. I thrive when given a real problem and the space to solve it my way — which, for the record, usually works. I'm not one for endless meetings or corporate theatre, but put me in front of a genuine challenge and I'm genuinely in my element.

I'm also deeply independent and adaptable — I can read a room, shift gears fast, and stay calm when things get complicated. I'm not the loudest person at the table, but I tend to be the one people turn to when the plan falls apart and someone needs to actually figure it out. No drama. Just results.

🛠️ Crafted. Analytical. Refreshingly direct. Occasionally too honest, but never boring.
